The playbook

four moves from booking to boarding
BOOK

Book the high-probability bank

Book the starred flight; keep the runners-up as backups. Avoid the flagged flights and the early-morning MAX-heavy bank entirely.

T-48h

Verify your actual tail — the single highest-leverage move

Aircraft assignments publish ~2 days out. Check your flight for a firm yes/no, or open the United app → flight status → inflight amenities (equipped flights list "Starlink Wi-Fi").

Check this flight ↗
T-24h

Re-check, and switch if you drew a dud

Swaps happen. If your flight drew a non-Starlink tail, ~3 departures a day on this route have confirmed Starlink tails — a same-day change (free with Gold+, else $75, waived to standby) converts your ~40–60% booking into near-certainty.

FLY

Connect free via MileagePlus

Starlink is free but you sign in with your MileagePlus account — make sure everyone traveling has one. Gate-to-gate, ~100–250 Mbps, VPN and video calls work. While you shop, the Google Flights Starlink extension badges equipped flights inline.
