The playbook

four moves from booking to boarding — works on any route
BOOK

Book the high-probability flight

Book the top-ranked flight above; keep the runners-up as backups. Avoid flights that typically draw 737 MAX, A319/A320 or 757 equipment — those fleets have zero Starlink installs today.

T-48h

Verify your actual tail — the single highest-leverage move

Aircraft assignments publish ~2 days out. Check your flight for a firm yes/no, or open the United app → flight status → inflight amenities (equipped flights list "Starlink Wi-Fi").

T-24h

Re-check, and switch if you drew a dud

Swaps happen. If your flight drew a non-Starlink tail, the confirmed-departures list above shows which flights on your route already have Starlink tails — a same-day change (free with Gold+, else $75, waived to standby) converts a coin-flip booking into near-certainty.

FLY

Connect free via MileagePlus

Starlink is free but you sign in with your MileagePlus account — make sure everyone traveling has one. Gate-to-gate, ~100–250 Mbps, VPN and video calls work. While you shop, the Google Flights Starlink extension badges equipped flights inline.
